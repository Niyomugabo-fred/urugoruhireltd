About Urugo Ruhire Ltd

Urugo Ruhire Ltd is a premier event decoration and ceremonial clothing company based in Kigali, Rwanda. We specialize in creating beautiful, elegant, and memorable experiences for all types of celebrations.

Founded with a passion for traditional Rwandan aesthetics combined with modern design principles, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in the event industry.

Our team of skilled decorators, designers, and stylists work tirelessly to ensure that every detail of your event is perfect, from the grand decorations to the finest details of your ceremonial attire.

With years of experience serving clients across Rwanda, we pride ourselves on our attention to detail, quality of service, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of elegant decoration and ceremonial wear services in Rwanda, known for our creativity, quality, and exceptional customer service.

We envision a world where every special moment is celebrated with beauty, style, and cultural significance, creating memories that last a lifetime.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional decoration and ceremonial wear services that exceed our clients' expectations, while preserving and celebrating Rwandan cultural heritage.

We are committed to delivering personalized, high-quality services that make every event special, memorable, and stress-free for our clients.

A Message from Our Founder

"When I started Urugo Ruhire, my vision was to create a company that celebrates the beauty of Rwandan traditions while providing modern, elegant solutions for life's most important celebrations. Today, I'm proud to see how we've grown and the joy we bring to our clients' special days. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do."
